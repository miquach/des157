Target Audience Test

Audience Tasks

  1. Read & click the alert.
  2. Put on VR viewer and navigate and look around the virtual reality space
  3. Examine the gaze-based input for the "about page."

Questions for Audience

  1. Did you understand what the webpage was about?
  2. How easy/difficult was it for you to access the virtual reality space?
  3. How do you feel about the dual screen for it to be adapted for mobile applications and VR goggles, like the Google Cardboard?
  4. How do you feel about the immersiveness of the webpage?
  5. Did the webpage feel like it was missing anything or as if it was missing anything?
